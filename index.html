01About

I'm a Tech Lead with eight years of platform and DevSecOps work behind me — built across fintech, healthcare, security, live streaming, and logistics. The thread isn't an industry; it's the kind of system that has to keep working when everything around it is changing.

Most recently, I lead the platform organisation at one of India's larger fintechs, supporting 200+ microservices and 200+ engineers across AWS, Oracle Cloud, and GCP. Before that I was running SRE and DevOps work at Innovaccer (healthcare data), Airtel Digital / Wynk (streaming), Sophos (cybersecurity), Rivigo (logistics), and Birdeye (SaaS).

I care about reliability that's actually rehearsed, costs that are owned rather than tolerated, and developer experience that doesn't depend on a human being on call to unblock it.
